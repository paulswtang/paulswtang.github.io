Stokes' theorem:

\\(\omega\\) is a differential form, \\(M\\) is a smooth manifold with boundary.

$$
\int_{\partial M} \omega = \int_M d\omega
$$

Green's theorem:

$$
\oint_C (L dx + M dy) = \iint \biggl( \frac{\partial M}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial L}{\partial y} \biggr) dx \, dy
$$
