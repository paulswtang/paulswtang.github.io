About Me.

It's quite reductive to try to pin a person down into a few key sentences. With that said, I'm quite interested in the following things:

  • Mathematics, especially branches with a geometric flavour.
  • Computer Science theory, especially computability, PL theory, type theory, and machine learning.
  • Semiotics, primarily applied towards industrial design. (One might infer my interest in colors from this page.)
  • Jazz, especially on piano (e.g., Thelonious Monk, Art Tatum) and synthesizer (e.g., Sun Ra).

I have formal training in the first two and the rest are hobbies (though I am not entirely incompetent at the piano).

I often do a bit of consulting, especially in regards to topics that are both mathematical and informatic in nature. To date, I have built: recommendation engines, front-end data analytics dashboards, RESTful APIs, analytics pipelines (in Hadoop, Storm, Spark, and more), and on-demand predictive frameworks. I am currently open to consulting contracts.
